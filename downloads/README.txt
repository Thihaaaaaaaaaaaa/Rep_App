Put your Android app file here as:  reps.apk

How to create it (free, ~5 min, once your site is live):
  1. Go to https://www.pwabuilder.com
  2. Enter your live site URL (e.g. https://your-domain.com) and click Start.
  3. Choose "Android" → "Generate Package".
  4. In the zip you download, find the signed .apk (app-release-signed.apk).
  5. Rename it to  reps.apk  and place it in THIS folder.

Once it's here, the "Download APK" button on /install turns on automatically.
Until then, Android users can still tap "Install app" (works with no file).

iPhone has no equivalent file — those users use "Add to Home Screen" in Safari.
