Install on iPhone & iPad

Apple doesn't allow downloadable app files, so Reps installs straight from Safari. It then opens full-screen, just like a normal app.

  1. Open this page in Safari (not Chrome) if you aren't already.
  2. Tap the Share button at the bottom of the screen.
  3. Scroll down and tap Add to Home Screen.
  4. Tap Add — the Reps icon appears on your home screen.
